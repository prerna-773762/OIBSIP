Courses
Computer Science and Engineering

The department was established in 1985 with.UG course and started a PG course in Computer Sci & Engg. in 2009-10. Research laboratory under the department is recognized research center of P.A.H.S.U.S. The academic curriculum covers a wide range of subjects like the computer programming, networking. Algorithms, Theory of Computation software engineering, distributed systems, network security, modern databases, Data Mining Big Data analytics and various other courses…

Information TEchnology

This Programme was established in 1999. The Programme is equipped with latest computer machines, installed with latest softwares. We have ten computer laboratories which are well equipped with latest terminals & peripherals and legal copies of latest software’s. These laboratories support an environment for Networking, Multimedia, Databases and advanced technologies like Mobile Application Development , AI & Machine Learning, Data Science and one laboratory is oriented for basic study of electronics and microprocessor.

Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering

The Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering Programme of Walchand Institute of Technology was established in the year 1999 with an intake of 40 seats. In 2002, the intake was increased to 60 seats while in 2010, the total intake of 120 seats was sanctioned. With the rapid advancement of the computer age and cybernetics, the boundaries between different branches of engineering have been steadily diminishing, particularly in the realm of “soft” engineering branches such as ‘ Electronics ‘, ‘ Electronics & Telecommunication ‘ and ‘ Computer Science & Engineering ‘.

Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering at Walchand Institute of Technology Solapur was established in the year 1987. The department offersB.Tech in Civil Engineering (UG Course) with Honors Degree in Infrastructure Engineering or Honors Degree in Sustainability Engineering,M.Tech. in Civil-Structural Engineering(P.G. Course), and Ph.D. in Civil Engineering (Doctoral Program).

Mechanical Engineering

The department has a vision to become a centre of excellence in the field of Mechanical and Automation Engineering. Departmental strength of highly qualified, experienced, and dedicated faculty along with creative students are working hard to achieve this goal. The objective of the department is to bridge the gap between industry and institute by incorporating the knowledge to the students of industrial automation like Manufacturing Automation, 3D Printing, Industry 4.0, Machine learning, Artificial Intelligence , Digital twin etc..
